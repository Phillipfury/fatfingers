Fat Fingers

The idea of this library is to handle cases when it's hard to determine what you clicked on. This is particularly a problem on Mobile, but also on any touch interface: fingers are fat, you don't get a preview of the target (the mouse pointer), and the finger covers the target making precision hard. So what is reasonably UI with a mouse can become very hard to navigate. Fat Fingers tries to detect imprecise actions on a page, and turn them into modal selections.
Some specific credit goes to Reddit Is Fun which provides an very modal interface on top of Reddit.
